AutocorrelationShell.ac2d — Method

ac2d(x, Lrow, Lcol, P, Q)

Computes autocorrelation wavelet coeficients for 2D signals. Returns a tensor (numrows, numcols, levelsofdecomp, levelsofdecomp) that stores the coefficients of the decomposed signal.

Arguments

  • x: 2D signals (n ,m) matrix
  • L_row: Decomposition level of rows
  • L_col: Decomposition level of columns
  • P: Low AC shell filter
  • Q: High AC shell filter
AutocorrelationShell.acwt2D — Method

acwt2d(x; Lrow, Lcol, P, Q)

Computes autocorrelation wavelet coeficients for 2D signals. Wrapper for ac2d. Returns a the tensor of (numrows, numcols, levelsofdecomp, levelsofdecomp) that stores the coefficients of the decomposed signal. Wrapper for the ac2d function.

Arguments

  • x: 2D signals (n ,m) matrix.
  • L_row: Decomposition level of rows. default: 1
  • L_col: Decomposition level of columns. default: 1
  • P: Low AC shell filter.
  • Q: High AC shell filter.
